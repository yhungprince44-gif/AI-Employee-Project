# Meeting #1 — Foundation

**Project:** AI Employee Project
**Employee:** Employee #1 — Business Research Analyst
**Status:** ✅ Complete

---

## 🎯 Mission

We are not building an app. We are building **Employee #1** — an AI-driven workflow that finds businesses that could benefit from a professional website and prepares everything needed for outreach.

## ✅ Decisions Made

- Employee #1's core responsibility: find businesses that are good website opportunities and prepare a complete report on each one.
- The project will be treated as building an "employee" with a defined job description — not a generic app or scraper.

## 📜 Rules Approved

1. Every employee has one primary responsibility.
2. AI follows our rules, not guesses.
3. Build reliable systems before automation.

## 💡 Ideas Discussed

**Employee #1's core tasks:**
1. Search for businesses.
2. Check if they have a website.
3. Review existing websites.
4. Flag businesses without websites.
5. Collect business information.
6. Produce a report.

**Information to collect per business:**
- Business name
- Industry
- Location
- Phone (public)
- Email (public)
- Website
- Social media
- Website status
- Improvement notes
- General notes
